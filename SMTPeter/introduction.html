Introduction

What is SMTPeter?

SMTPeter is a cloud-based SMTP server that delivers your email fast and secure. You can connect your own application or mail server through SMTP or by using our REST API and start sending your emails right away. All you have to do is connect!

Who is SMTPeter for?

SMTPeter is for anyone looking for professional email delivery. Whether they are transactional or marketing emails, SMTPeter is made to deliver large volumes of email and gives back detailed statistics of your campaigns.

Easy to integrate

Connecting your own application to SMTPeter and using the SMTP relay service is easy. Simply create an account to get your login credentials and configure your application to forward your emails through SMTP or by using the REST API.

Setting up your account

Once you have created your account you can generate your own SMTPeter login credentials. These credentials allow you to connect to SMTPeter and have to be included whenever you want to connect to the SMTPeter server to send emails. Connecting to SMTPeter

As we said before, connecting to SMTPeter is easy. Simply login and connect through SMTP or use our REST API. For more information about connecting to SMTPeter you can read our connecting documentation.
