Introduction

What is SMTPeter?

SMTPeter is a cloud-based SMTP server for fast and secure email delivery. You can connect your own application or mail server through SMTP or by using our REST API and start sending your emails right away. All you have to do is connect!

Who is SMTPeter for?

SMTPeter is for anyone looking for professional email delivery. Whether they are transactional or marketing emails, SMTPeter is made to deliver large volumes of email messages and returns detailed statistics of your campaigns.

Easy to integrate

Connecting your own application to SMTPeter and using the SMTP relay service is easy. Simply create an account to get your login credentials and configure your application to forward your emails through SMTP or by using the REST API.

Setting up your account

Once you have created your account you can generate your own SMTPeter login credentials. These credentials allow you to connect to SMTPeter and have to be included whenever you want to connect to the SMTPeter server to send emails. Connecting to SMTPeter

As we said before, connecting to SMTPeter is easy. Simply login with your SMTPeter credentials and connect through SMTP or use our REST API. For more information about connecting to SMTPeter you can read our connecting documentation.

A single account for all of Copernica's services

Once you have created your account you can use the account to log in to all of Copernica's services. If you want to use an easy to use editor to create and send responsive emails or connect to our responsive API, you can log in to ResponsiveEmail.com. Or if you prefer to send the emails yourself, you can login, download and install MailerQ. MailerQ is the SMTP server software SMTPeter is built on. If you are looking for email marketing software which allows you to create, send and automate your email campaigns you can log in Copernica Marketing Software.
